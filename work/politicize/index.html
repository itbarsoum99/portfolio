Politicize

Challenge

To create a simple, modern website to present daily, unbiased national news briefings. The website would feature briefings by email and on the site, focusing on important non-headline news and nonpartisan interpretations of the news. The website would need to be user-friendly and article-based.

Role

I performed web design and development, UI/UX design, privacy policy advisement, and content development for Politicize, the briefing service.

Results

Politicize began publishing daily news briefings in June 2021 on its site. It has a content-centric site with a dark theme and simple, easy-access buttons and links.
