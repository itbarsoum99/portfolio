closing the digital divide

Web
Design
Camps

Challenge

To create a service organization to bridge the digital divide that would provide live, virtual 21st century internet education to young people everywhere. It would attract a variety of students at a relatively low cost, all of which would go towards E2D, an organization committed to ending the digital-access gap.

Role

I founded the organization and designed its website, while developing a modern, student-centric curriculum to teach web design. I set up the fundraiser and taught the inaugural course in summer 2021, with plans to continue the program in future years.

Results

The inaugural iteration of the camps in June 2021 was a success, with four students participating and $200 raised. Students had positive outcomes and learned a tremendous amount in the span of a week.
