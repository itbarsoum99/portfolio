Challenge

To learn the Swift programming language and build an app that would drastically improve student productivity. The app would provide a variety of features at no cost, including notes, an agenda, and todo lists. The app would be designed for functionality as opposed to appearance (UX over UI), giving it a simplistic yet utilitarian feel.

Role

For my IB MYP Personal Project, I taught myself Swift using resources on the internet. I performed app design, content development, and feature coordination for the app, guiding it from start to finish. I did everything from sketching to building.
