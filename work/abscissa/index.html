visual journalism

Abscissa

Challenge

To create a sleek, necessary visual journalism website that would present data-based content in attractive ways. The website would cover a variety of topics and have several specialized "features." But since data-based journalism is often presented in non-accessible ways, the content would also have to be easy to access and understand.

Role

I performed front-end development, content creation and presentation, logo design, and branding for Abscissa, the novel visual-journalism site.

Results

The result is a smooth, minimalistic design with beautiful fonts; simple, elegant logo design; bright, eye-catching colors; and accessible, easy-to-understand content.
