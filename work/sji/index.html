Challenge

There are a variety of causes worth supporting in the early 21st century: Black Lives Matter, LGBTQ+ equality, climate action, and women's rights, to name a few. My Social Justice Initiative is a dashboard for change in turbulent times. It would bring together a variety of important organizations in one place and ask visitors to commit to making change in their communities.

Role

I created my Social Justice Initiative in spring 2021 to consolidate major important issues into one launchpoint for change. I performed web design and content development for the initiative.

Results

The initiative accomplishes its mission: to be an accessible, 21st-century launchpoint for social change and societal progress. The website offers cool tones and subtle engagement to help the visitor find what they need to contribute to progress.
